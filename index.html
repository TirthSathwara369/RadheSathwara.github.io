But What If!

What if gravity isn’t what we think it is? What if time bends differently inside your body? What if the universe is listening? At But What If, we don’t settle for the answers we’re given — we explore the possibilities nobody dared to ask. Founded by Tirth Sathwara, a young theorist and science rebel, this is more than just a research platform. It’s a playground of bold ideas, daring questions, and independent scientific thinking. Every theory here begins not with “This is,” but with “But what if...?”

About me

Young Scientist & Researcher

Hi, I’m Tirth Sathwara, a curious teenager from India who’s trying to understand the universe in my own way. I don’t have a lab, a degree, or formal recognition. What I do have is questions — a lot of them — and a strong need to explore them. I’ve always felt that the universe might work in ways that go beyond what textbooks show us. That’s why I started writing my own theories, especially about fluid spacetime, black holes, wormholes, and how everything might be connected through hidden forces we don’t fully understand yet. I’m not here to prove I’m right. I’m here because thinking differently is the only way we’ve ever moved forward. I made this website so I could share my ideas, not because they’re perfect, but because they’re honest. If someone reads them, questions them, improves them, or even proves them wrong — then I’ll know I did something meaningful. This is just the beginning.
